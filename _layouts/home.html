Who we are

REdy is a Pan-African movement, dedicated to educating and empowering people in over 45 countries in Africa. We use the democratising power of the internet to connect and build a community of Africans who, like us, believe that the future is Africa’s!

What We Do

REdy and our partners launch high profile information campaigns and inspire action through education in Human Rights, Culture, Law and Governance, Health & Medicine, Home & Family, Environment, Business and Science & Tech. Our content is specifically written for our African audience, paying special respect to and incorporating their values and culture in all our work.

Our Approach

Harnessing Africa’s Digital Revolution

Education goes beyond the classroom and that instigates independent thought and self-efficacy. We use digital technology to engage with and reach out to the furthest corners of Africa, making high quality African-centric content available for free to everyone.

Equal Opportunities for All

All Africans have the right to equal opportunity to live an educated, healthy and financially successful and independent life.

Free Access to Content

We are an official partner of Facebook internet.org’s Free Basics platform. Our materials are available without data charge to an ever-increasing population that is connected to the internet and to vital information through Facebook's services.
